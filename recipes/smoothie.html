Cherry Smoothie

Description

Nothing beats a delicious, colorful smoothie on a hot day!

Ingredients

  • Two Cups Spinach
  • Two Tablespoons Cocoa Powder
  • 1 Cup Frozen Cherries
  • 1 Tablespoon Chia Seeds
  • 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1 Cup Oatmilk
  • 1 Cup Ice

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients together in a blender
  2. Blend ingredients until the mixture is smooth
  3. Pour the mixture into a glass and enjoy!
