Scrambled Eggs

Description

These are the greatest scrambled eggs you will ever have. The best part is they take less than ten minutes to make, and do not require many ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon Heavy Cream
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together 3 large eggs, 1 tablespooon of heavy cream, and 1 teaspoon of salt until mixed
  2. Melt 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat, until you hear a soft sizzle
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and swirl to coat the bottom of the pan
  4. After 2 to 3 minutes, the eggs will start to form large curds
